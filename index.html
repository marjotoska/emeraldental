Welcome to Emerald Dental Clinic

Emerald Dental is a modern dental clinic conveniently located within 5 minutes from the city center of the capital Tirana.

We are specialized in advanced diagnostics and treatment of dental and oral disorders. In addition to high-end dental equipment, all services are provided in a comfortable, luxury environment. New patients are welcomed with a complimentary oral health consultation.

The treatments we offer include general and advanced dental procedures, including, but not limited to:

  • General Dentistry
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Oral Surgery
  • Dental Implants
  • Root Canals
  • Wisdom Teeth Extraction/Treatment
  • Teeth Extractions
  • Dental Crowns
  • Bridges
  • Veneers
  • Braces/ Invisalign
  • Preventive Care
  • Periodontal Treatment
  • Dental Emergency Treatment

You can also check out our social medias where we regularly post about our patients' experience!
